About the Project

SkillSwap is a peer-to-peer learning platform developed as a capstone project by Group 13, a team of Computer Science Engineering students. The project represents a full-stack web application built over the course of an academic semester, incorporating modern technologies, thoughtful UI/UX design, and a strong research foundation drawn from 15 peer-reviewed papers in the field of collaborative and peer-led learning.

We built SkillSwap because we believe that everyone has something valuable to teach and something new to learn — and that technology should make those exchanges easier, more engaging, and more accessible for everyone. We welcome feedback, collaboration ideas, and any questions about the platform.
